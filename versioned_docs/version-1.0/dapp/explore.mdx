To check the products that are registered on Wireshape blockchain, go to: https://staging.wireshape.org/explore

You can perform searches by product name, brand, feature or GTIN. You can also apply filters such as the country in which the product was registered and category.

By clicking on the Show Product button you will be able to see all the registered data of the product, as well as know which contributor was responsible for registering the last version and the date of registration on the blockchain. It is also possible to check all transaction details on the blockchain by clicking on the button Check on Block Explorer.
