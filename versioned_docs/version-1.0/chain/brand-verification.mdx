## Introduction
The company will be able to carry out data applications of its products, being validated by the community, they will receive the seal of data verified by the brand. The Verified Brand will also be able to validate products of its brand that are already registered on the blockchain. In addition, the Verified Brand will be allowed to enter extra data, such as logistics and user manual.
